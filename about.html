Why This Site Exists

I've been playing GTA Online since launch day 2013. I've watched every major update drop, every meta shift, every Rockstar nerf that wrecked a perfectly optimized grind. I know how much time gets wasted in the first weeks of a new GTA era by players who didn't understand the economy before they started spending.

When GTA 6 was confirmed, I started building the resource I wished had existed when GTA Online launched a single place where every money method is tracked, ranked, and filtered for your actual situation. Not generic tips. Not clickbait tier lists. Real data, updated as fast as the meta shifts.
